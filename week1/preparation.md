# Preparation
This weeks preperation is more minimal than normally, but you can try to google the topics "serverless" and "benefits of moving into the cloud" as much as you find relevant. There are also videos, discussions and other materials online. 

Main thing you should do: **Think of three reasons why you want to use Serverless when creating IT solutions.**, and the find **one job application which you think this course could help you achieve**.  

Then, please read through the [lesson plan](https://github.com/HackYourFuture-CPH/hyf-serverless-course/blob/main/week1/lesson-plan.md).
